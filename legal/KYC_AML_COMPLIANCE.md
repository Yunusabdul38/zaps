# KYC/AML Compliance Documentation

**Last Updated: [Date]**

## 1. Compliance Statement
Zaps is committed to complying with applicable Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and Counter-Terrorist Financing (CTF) regulations, including FinCEN guidance in the United States and similar frameworks globally.

## 2. Customer Due Diligence (CDD)
Depending on the volume and type of transactions processed through Zaps, users and merchants may be subject to Customer Due Diligence. This includes:
- Verification of identity (e.g., government-issued ID).
- Verification of address (e.g., utility bill).
- Verification of business entity status for merchants.

## 3. Transaction Monitoring
We employ automated systems to monitor transactions for suspicious activity. If anomalous behavior is detected, Zaps reserves the right to suspend or restrict accounts pending further investigation.

## 4. Reporting
Zaps will file Suspicious Activity Reports (SARs) with relevant financial authorities when legally required to do so.
