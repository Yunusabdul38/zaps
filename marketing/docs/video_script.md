# App Preview Video Script (30-60 Seconds)

## Concept
A high-energy, fast-paced video showcasing the core value prop of Zaps: instant, secure payments.

## Script

**[0:00 - 0:05] Hook**
- *Visual:* Fast cuts of people struggling with traditional crypto wallets (typing long addresses, waiting for confirmations).
- *Text on Screen:* "Crypto payments used to be slow..."
- *Audio:* A low, dramatic bass tone that stops abruptly with a record scratch.

**[0:05 - 0:15] The Solution**
- *Visual:* The sleek Zaps logo appears. Cut to a user opening the app with FaceID. 
- *Voiceover:* "Meet Zaps. The non-custodial wallet built for the real world."
- *Text on Screen:* INSTANT. SECURE. YOURS.

**[0:15 - 0:30] Feature Showcase: Paying**
- *Visual:* A user is at a coffee shop. The barista turns an iPad around with a Zaps QR code. The user scans it with their phone. 
- *UI Close-up:* The payment screen shows "Tap to Zap". A green checkmark instantly appears. 
- *Voiceover:* "Pay instantly with QR codes or NFC."

**[0:30 - 0:45] Feature Showcase: Merchants**
- *Visual:* Split screen. Left side: Consumer app. Right side: Merchant Dashboard showing a new transaction popping up instantly.
- *Voiceover:* "And for businesses, track everything in real-time with zero hidden fees."

**[0:45 - 0:60] Outro / Call to Action**
- *Visual:* The app icon sits center screen with App Store and Google Play badges below it. 
- *Voiceover:* "Take control of your assets. Download Zaps today."
- *Text on Screen:* Available now on iOS & Android. Zaps.com
