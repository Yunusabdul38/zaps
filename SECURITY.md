## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of Zaps very seriously. If you believe you have found a security vulnerability in our smart contracts, backend infrastructure, or mobile application, please report it to us immediately.

### How to Report
Please email our security team directly at **security@zaps.com** rather than opening a public issue.

In your email, please include:
- A description of the vulnerability.
- Steps to reproduce the issue.
- Potential impact (e.g., funds at risk, data exposure).
- Proof of concept (PoC) code if applicable.

### Bug Bounty Program
Zaps operates a Bug Bounty program to reward independent security researchers who find and responsibly disclose vulnerabilities.
- **Critical Smart Contract Bugs:** Up to $50,000
- **High Severity Backend/Mobile Bugs:** Up to $10,000
- **Medium Severity:** Up to $2,000

*Note: Rewards are paid out in stablecoins or native tokens at the discretion of the Zaps security team.*

### Response Times
We will acknowledge receipt of your vulnerability report within 24 hours and strive to provide a preliminary assessment within 48 hours.

Please do not publicly disclose the vulnerability until we have had sufficient time to patch it and notify our users.
